Brownies

Ingredients

  • 55% Cacao dark chocolate
  • 160g of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 20g of bitter cocoa in powder form
  • 150g of butter
  • 90g of 00 flour
  • 170g of already peeled hazelnuts
  • 1 pinch of salt

How to prepare the dish

  1. Melt the dark chocolate.

  2. Melt the butter.

  3. Mix the chocolate and the butter.

  4. Cook the hazelnuts in a pan.

  5. In a container mix the eggs and the sugar,
    mounting with electric whisks - while adding the salt

  6. When ready, join with the mixture of chocolate and butter - continuing mounting.
    Sift in the flour and the cocoa in. Continue to mount with whisks,
    until everything is homogeneous.

  7. Add the hazelnuts.

  8. Cook in the static oven for 25 minutes, maintaining 180 Celsius degrees and:

Enjoy ;-)
