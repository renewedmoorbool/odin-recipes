Pasta with prawns

Replicate the magical taste of pasta with prawns abiding by the following guidelines.

It will drive you in a world of taste.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Prawns
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put in a pan the oil
  2. Heat it
  3. When it's hot put the prawns
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Add tomatoes
  6. When everything is dense add the already boiled pasta
