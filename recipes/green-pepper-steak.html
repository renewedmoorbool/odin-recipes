Green pepper steak

A steak which is prepared for those who have a good taste.
If you wish you were in heaven, you don't have to anymore.

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg of beef steak
  • 80 g of liquid fresh cream
  • 100 g of beef broth
  • 20 g of clarified butter
  • 00 Flour
  • Salt
  • 20 g of green pepper
  • 40 g of mustard
  • 25 g of brandy

How to prepare the dish

  1. Prepare the beef broth, by diluting beef cubes
    into hot boiling water.

  2. Take the steak and flour the top and the bottom surfaces. The sides have to remain clean.

  3. Liquefy the clarified butter in a pan.
    When hot, put the steak above the butter.

  4. When cooked enough, simmer with brandy until reduced.
    The brandy will probably catch fire, it is part of the process.
    Be careful while handling the pan:

    You don't want to spill out the burning alcohol, again. For the third time in a row. In the same cooking session.

  5. Now that the flame is out, hopefully, add the green pepper pieces in the pan and, also, the salt and the mustard.

  6. Cook everything for a more few minutes. Then, add 50 g of liquid fresh cream and 50 g of beef broth.
    Mix things with a spoon and, then, put the liquid on top of the steak.

    Turn off the flame and:

Enjoy ;-)
